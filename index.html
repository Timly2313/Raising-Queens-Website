About Us

Raising Queens Foundation is a non-profit organisation that empowers underprivileged women and youth by developing strategies for their educational and personal development. The organisation encourages woman and the youth to view higher education as a desirable and important tool for their empowerment. The primary focus of the organisation is advancing gender equality and basic human rights including providing feminine hygiene and reproductive health essentials, facilitating educational workshops, and mentoring the youth. The organisation is founded by a young woman and is led by young individuals who empower other young individuals
